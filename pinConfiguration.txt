Arduino - A0, A1, A2, A3 > NodeMCU - D5, D6, D7, D8
--------------------------------------------------------
LCD - SCL > Arduino A5
    - SDA > Arduino A4
--------------------------------------------------------
DHT11 - DATA > Arduino D4
--------------------------------------------------------
Soil Moisture - AnalogSignal > Arduino ~D3
--------------------------------------------------------
Light Intensity - (voltage divider) > Arduino ~D5
--------------------------------------------------------
Relay Module - EN1 > Arduino > D7
	     - EN2 > Arduino > D8
--------------------------------------------------------
